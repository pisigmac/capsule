# Payments

## Overview
Capsule uses Stripe for subscription billing.

## Supported Methods
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex)
- PayPal
- Wire transfer (Enterprise)

## Billing Cycle
- Monthly or annual (20% discount)
- Pro-rated upgrades/downgrades
- 30-day refund policy

## Invoicing
- Automatic receipts via email
- VAT/GST support
- Enterprise: custom invoicing
